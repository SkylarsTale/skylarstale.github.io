Episode 1



"The Astral Express! I can see it on the horizon!" Asta said, smiling.
"I can't wait to get on!" April said.
"What's that dot over there?" Asta cried, pointing to a small dot, slowly increasing in size.
"It's a comet!" April said. As the dot came closer, the two girls realised it wasn't a comet, but a girl, hurtling towards the station.

She lay there. On the metal.
"Hello?" Asta said, trying to be as slow and as clear as she could be.
The girl groaned.
"Who are you?" she asked.
"I'm Asta," Asta said. "Wait a second." She tapped her wrist a few times, and while she tapped, April said,
"Your mental state is being temporarily ajusted to calm you. Please remain calm."
"April!" Asta said, sternly."Her name is Skylar. She's 16 years old, my age."
"Wow, big sis! How do you know all of that?" April asked.
"She's not human. She's a hologram. Hard light." Asta said.
"Now what do we do?" April asked.

Take her to the Medical Bay.

Take her to our room.
